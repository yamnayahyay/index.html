05 — The Human

Beyond the
Resume

What I value in teams
Quality over speed — but never one without the other
  • Intellectual honesty in hiring decisions
  • People who raise the bar just by being in the room
  • Speed that doesn't cut corners on culture
  • Data-informed but human-led decisions
  • Leaders who trust their talent partners
How I think about hiring
Hiring is product design for your team

Every hire is a system input. Get the inputs wrong and no amount of culture work or OKRs will fix the output. I think about hiring the way a PM thinks about feature design — define the problem clearly, design the evaluation rigorously, measure everything.

Working style
Operator mindset. Founder energy.

I operate at the intersection of strategy and execution. Boardroom workforce planning in the morning, deep in LinkedIn Recruiter in the afternoon. I built my CRM in Replit. I build Custom GPTs. I stay hands-on because the craft matters.

Outside the work
Curious about how organisations scale

I follow the startup ecosystem closely, especially MENA. I mentor early-career HR professionals. I'm genuinely excited by AI's role in hiring — not as a replacement for judgment, but as infrastructure that amplifies it.
