02 — Evaluation Framework
Bar Raiser Hiring Framework
Structured evaluation system maintaining quality at 3× hiring velocity — calibrated rubrics, designated Bar Raisers and rigorous debrief processes.
Process DesignQualityStructure
🎯
The Problem

As Markaz scaled from 40 to 130+ people, interview consistency broke down. Different interviewers applied different standards and offer decisions became slow and subjective.

The System Design

Adapted Amazon's Bar Raiser model for an early-stage startup: standardised competency matrices per level and function, calibrated scoring rubrics, designated Bar Raiser roles per interview panel with veto authority on culture-diluting hires, and a structured forced-ranking debrief process.

Tools Used
GreenhouseNotionBrightHireAshby
Measured Impact
90% offer acceptance sustained through 3× hiring velocity. Quality-of-hire measurably improved. The framework remained in use after tenure ended.
03 — Recruiting Operations
TA Analytics Dashboards
Real-time Power BI dashboards giving leadership full visibility into funnel health, pipeline velocity and hiring conversion across all active roles.
Power BIDataOperations
📊
The Problem

Leadership had no real-time hiring visibility. Decisions came from gut feel and delayed spreadsheets — by the time a problem surfaced in the data, it was already a multi-week blocker.

The System Design

Built live dashboards in Power BI and Looker Studio tracking pipeline velocity by stage, funnel conversion per role type, time-to-offer versus target, source effectiveness by channel and interviewer throughput. Embedded into weekly leadership syncs as a standing agenda item.

Tools Used
Power BILooker StudioGreenhouseAshbyGoogle Sheets
Measured Impact
Time-to-hire reduced 23% in the quarter following adoption. Leadership shifted from monthly retrospective reporting to live hiring visibility. Pipeline blockers identified 2–3 weeks earlier.
04 — Pipeline Management
Candidate Nurture CRM
Built on Replit — a sequenced candidate relationship system keeping warm pipelines alive and reactivating strong candidates when the right role opens.
CRMNurtureReplit
🌱
The Problem

Strong candidates who weren't ready to move were being lost entirely. Every new role started cold from scratch, wasting weeks of sourcing budget with no retained pipeline memory.

The System Design

Built a candidate relationship system using Replit as the backend. Warm pipelines segmented by function, seniority and readiness signal. Automated but personalised outreach cadences timed to 30/60/90-day reactivation windows, with engagement scoring across all touchpoints.

Tools Used
ReplitGemLinkedIn RecruiterNotion
Measured Impact
Passive talent conversion increased 15%. Reactivated pipeline became a consistent source of placements within 60 days of a role opening.
